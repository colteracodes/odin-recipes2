Lasagna

This lasagna is a treasured family recipe passed down from my grandmother, whose love for cooking shines through every layer. With its rich and savory flavors, it features perfectly cooked sheets of pasta, generous layers of seasoned ground beef, and a creamy, cheesy filling made from ricotta and mozzarella. Each bite is a delightful combination of textures, with the pasta providing a tender bite and the meat adding a hearty depth.

What truly makes this lasagna special is the homemade marinara sauce, simmered to perfection with fresh herbs, garlic, and just the right amount of spices. It’s baked until golden and bubbly, creating a tantalizing aroma that fills the kitchen and invites everyone to the table. This dish is more than just a meal; it’s a comforting reminder of family gatherings, laughter, and the love that goes into every bite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to the beef, cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread a portion of the cheese mixture, followed by a layer of the beef mixture and more marinara sauce.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of noodles topped with marinara sauce and the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
