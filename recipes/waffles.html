Protein Banana Waffles

These protein banana waffles are a delicious and nutritious way to start your day! Made with ripe bananas and a blend of protein powder, these waffles are not only fluffy and satisfying but also packed with the energy you need to tackle your morning. Theyâ€™re perfect for busy mornings when you want something quick yet wholesome.

The sweetness of the bananas combined with the rich flavor of protein powder creates a delightful breakfast option that will keep you full for hours. You can top these waffles with fresh fruit, a drizzle of maple syrup, or a dollop of Greek yogurt for added flavor and nutrition. Enjoy these waffles as a healthy treat any time of the day!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 oz ground flax seeds
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened plain almond milk
  • 1 cup protein powder (your choice)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mash the ripe bananas until smooth.
  2. Add the eggs and mix until well combined.
  3. Stir in the ground flax seeds and almond milk, mixing until smooth.
  4. Gradually add the protein powder and flour to the mixture, stirring until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Preheat your waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  6. Pour an appropriate amount of batter into the preheated waffle iron and cook until golden brown, about 4-5 minutes.
  7. Carefully remove the waffles and repeat with the remaining batter.
  8. Serve warm with your favorite toppings. Enjoy!
