Baked Salmon

This baked salmon recipe is a delicious and healthy dish that brings out the natural flavors of the fish. It’s simple to prepare, making it perfect for busy weeknights or special occasions. The salmon is seasoned with fresh herbs and a hint of lemon, ensuring each bite is moist, flaky, and packed with flavor.

What makes this recipe stand out is the combination of garlic, dill, and lemon zest, which elevates the taste while allowing the salmon to shine. Baked to perfection, the salmon is a nutritious choice that pairs well with a variety of sides, from roasted vegetables to fluffy rice. Enjoy this easy yet elegant dish that’s sure to impress family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped (or 1 teaspoon dried dill)
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon slices for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it with cooking spray.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, minced garlic, dill, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Place the salmon fillets skin-side down on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Brush the olive oil mixture generously over each salmon fillet.
  6. Top each fillet with a lemon slice for added flavor.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  8. Remove from the oven and let rest for a couple of minutes before serving. Enjoy!
